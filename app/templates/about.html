About

Time To Convert is a tiny tool that gives a simple green/amber/red signal for currency conversion timing based on recent exchange rates. It’s a historical heuristic, not financial advice.

How it works

  • Looks at the last 180 days (configurable)
  • Computes the 50th and 75th percentiles of rates for the selected pair
  • Compares today’s rate to those thresholds to classify the signal
